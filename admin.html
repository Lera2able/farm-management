Admin Instructions

  1. Pending Additions: Approve or reject new livestock numbers submitted by farm workers
  2. Pending Registrations: Approve livestock that have been sold or died
  3. Google Sheets: View full attendance history and manage data
  4. Local Data Export: Download all locally stored attendance records

Approving Additions:

When you approve a new livestock number:

  • It will be added to the main livestock list
  • It will appear in the Google Sheets master list
  • Farm workers will see it in their attendance app

Approving Sold/Died:

When you approve a status change:

  • The livestock will be marked as inactive (red)
  • It will move to the "Not Active" group
  • It will no longer count in active attendance
  • The record will be saved in the "Sold/Died Register" sheet
